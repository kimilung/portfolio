_NAME

Ali'i Kula Lavender Farm

_TYPE

UI/UX Design

_OBJECTIVE

The objective of this project was to redesign Ali'i Kula Lavender Farm's website, making the user experience easier while still being educationally informative. The new design targets a broader audience: attracting prospective visitors and maintaining loyal consumers alike. It showcases the beauty of AKL's location, as well as, provides useful customer testimony about the products, events, tours, and overall experience.

_CLIENT

School Project

_TECHNOLOGY

Adobe InDesign & Photoshop

_AWARDS

Published by the Board of Student Publications in the Leahi Creative Arts Journal
