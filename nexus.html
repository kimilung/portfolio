_NAME

Nexus: Portfolio & Demo Reel

_TYPE

Print &s; UI/UX Design

_CLIENT

New Media Arts Cohort 2018

_OBJECTIVE

To build a “brand” that portrayed and reflected the students of the 2018 New Media Arts program at Kapi’olani Community College—creating promotional and web collateral that showcased the learnings of the program.

_ROLE

Email Blast (Designer/Developer/Motion Graphics)
Physical Invitations (Designer/Illustrator)
Portfolio Show (Event Planner, Imagineer)
Website (Designer/Developer/Photographer)

_TECHNOLOGY

Adobe Animate, InDesign, Illustrator
CSS, HTML, JavaScript/JQuery
Mail Chimp

_TEAM

Allyson Villanueva, Chris Gargiulo, Emily Moody

_DRAWING INSPIRATION

We drew inspiration from the theme of “connect the dots”, connecting our educational knowledge to any future projects that may come our way. However, not wanting to be another cliché, we expanded our idea and explored the world of “connectivity”—from nuerons in our brains to chain reactions caused by an event (like the big bang theory), we mind mapped our way through and decided to go with a spacial theme that melded all our thoughts together…behold:
NEXUS: A connection or series of connections linking two or more things. A connected group or series. The central most important point or place.
