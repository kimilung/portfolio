_Positioning Statement

The Fold is a new pop-up experience—bringing figments of our imagination to life through the versatile medium of paper. In comparison to its “strict” art museum counterparts—The Fold invites its guests to interact with its exhibits—immersing them in a “world of pure imagination”.

_Pattern
_Brand Personality

A modern experience—we marry the educational opportunities of the past with the interactive applications of today—creating a world full of fantasy and wonder.

_Imagery
_Audience

Although The Fold targets those engaged in social media, aiming to create a very “Instagram-able” exhibition, they are keen to not neglect children and their parents. Offering family-fun days to art after dark events, there is an experience for everyone ages 5–65.

_Tone of Voice

Think beyond the sheet. Go beyond the fold.
