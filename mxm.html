_NAME

The Met: Manus x Machina Brochure

_TYPE

Print Design

_OBJECTIVE

The assignment for this project was to create a promotional brochure for The Met. Covering the Manus x Machina: Fashion in an Age of Technology exhibition, the design goal was to layout all the content and imagery in a way that reflects the unique style of fashion versus technology. Using typography, grid layout, and imagery I was able to achieve this through making appropriate design decisions.

_CLIENT

School Project

_TECHNOLOGY

Adobe InDesign & Photoshop
