_Positioning Statement

Floristry celebrates craft, longevity, and exemplary service. Whether it be connecting with the human emotion through the manner of gifting flowers or putting together a little something for yourself, our worldly assortment will help to convey the perfect message through the act of science. And though science may not seem all that appealing, our florals are classic and hold a modern appeal—they are sure to capture the eyes and hearts of many.

_Brand Personality

Though we are classic in nature—drawing upon natural scents and beauty—we bring a modern creativity to our design. Excuding dexterity in our craft, we always put the customers experience first—bringing to life our clients imagination.

_Audience

From their youthful mid-20’s to experienced 50’s, our cosmopolitan client values design and knowledge. Whether it be the DIY ingenue creating her next masterpiece or the thoughtful spouse wanting to impact the otherhalfs day, we interact with each client on an personalized level.
